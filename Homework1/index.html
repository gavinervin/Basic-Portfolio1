About Me

My full name is Thomas Gavin Ervin, but I go by Gavin. I was born in Picayune, MS and grew up outside of Jackson, MS. After high school, I moved to North Carolina to attend Culinary school where I received a degree in restaurant managment. I had a passion for cooking and loved the high pace of the restaurant industry. After several years managing various kitches around the country, I decided it was time to go back to school and find a career with less stress and less physical demand.

Shortly after moving back to NC for school, I met my amazing wife, Tracy. We moved to Durham, NC in mid 2015 so I could complete my accounting degree at NC State, which I am currently finishing up. I began the coding bootcamp to add a new skill to possibly mesh with my accounting knowledge, plus I have always loved all things computers and gaming.
